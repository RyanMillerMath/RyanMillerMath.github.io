I'm Ryan Miller. Welcome to my website!

I'm a junior software engineer, technological tinkerer, and mathematical fanatic. My background is in mathematics and computer science with an academic interest in software design, abstract algebra, and graph theory and a personal interest in programming principles, symbolic logic, and the history of mathematics. My main interest and ambition in software development is to become a master of automation, as I firmly believe automation will define the 21st century as mass production and manufacturing defined the 20th.

My deepest passion is learning. I'm routinely trying to fulfill my insatiable curiosity. Naturally, I'm constantly reading anything and everything, from coding to mathematics to biology to history. Recently, I've decided to teach myself mechanical and electrical engineering and, as a result, picked up a hobby of dismantling old computers, TVs, and really anything I can dig up to figure out how all the components work together. In my additional free time, I'm an avid gamer, cinema lover, and recreational musician.
